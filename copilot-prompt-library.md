## Guardrails

- Do not paste personal health information (PHI) or personally identifiable information (PII) into AI prompts.
- Do not include secrets, unpublished results, or internal identifiers.
- Remove or anonymize confidential information before use.

## Red Flag Checklist

- Does the text include names or IDs? Remove or anonymize them.
- Does it contain unpublished data or confidential methods? Omit or generalize.
- Are there internal project codes or identifiers? Replace them with placeholders.

## Sanitize‑First Steps

1. Skim the source material and redact sensitive information.
2. Summarize or paraphrase details instead of copying verbatim.
3. Confirm that the prompt does not expose confidential data before sending it to an AI assistant.
